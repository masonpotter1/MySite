Privacy

Cloutsites may use privacy-friendly analytics to understand aggregate site performance, popular pages, and basic conversion paths. The goal is operational insight, not resale of personal data.

The launch intake form is front-end only and opens an email draft. If you contact Cloutsites, your message will be used to respond to your inquiry and scope the requested work.

Future production intake integrations should add server-side validation, spam protection, retention rules, and CRM/email processing disclosures before collecting leads directly.
